## ğŸ“ How to Use This Guide

### For Self-Study

1. **Read sequentially:** Follow the roadmap chapter by chapter
2. **Do exercises:** Complete exercises after each chapter
3. **Practice:** Apply what you learn to real features
4. **Ask questions:** Note questions and ask your team lead

### For Mentoring

1. **Assign chapters:** Give chapters to the junior developer
2. **Review exercises:** Check their exercise answers
3. **Provide feedback:** Review their test cases and bug reports
4. **Pair testing:** Do exploratory testing together

1. **Templates:** Team can use templates for consistency
2. **Examples:** Reference examples when needed

## â“ FAQ

### How long will this take?

**Minimum:** 4-6 weeks for basics  
**Comprehensive:** 3-6 months to become proficient  
**Mastery:** Ongoing practice

### Do I need to complete everything?

**No!** Focus on what's relevant to your role:
- **Manual QA:** Focus on Chapters 1-5
- **Automation QA:** Add Chapter 6
- **QA Lead:** All chapters + strategy

### Can I skip chapters?

**Not recommended** for first-time learning. Each chapter builds on previous ones. However, you can:
- Review chapters you already know quickly
- Deep dive into areas you need most

### What if I get stuck?

1. **Re-read** the relevant section
2. **Check examples** for similar scenarios
3. **Ask your mentor** or team lead
4. **Practice** with simpler scenarios first
